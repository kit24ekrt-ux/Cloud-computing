About Me

Hello! I'm Karuna Tamang, a passionate designer and developer based in Kathmandu, Nepal. I specialise in creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional.

My work sits at the intersection of design and technology — I care deeply about every pixel, every interaction, and every line of code. I believe great software should feel effortless to use.

When I'm not building things for the web, you'll find me exploring the mountains of Nepal, reading about design philosophy, or experimenting with new creative tools.
